Tuesday Morning Routine

Phase 1: Kitchen Reset & Packing 6:30 AM - 7:00 AM
  • Logistics: Bag pre-made lunch and stage it with work gear.
  • Dishwasher & Sink: Empty clean dishes, scrape and load breakfast plates, rinse sink.
  • Surface Clear: Wipe down counters and stow open cereal boxes or ingredients.
Phase 2: Personal Hygiene 7:00 AM - 7:30 AM
  • Prep: Shower, shave, and get dressed for the office.
  • Tidy: Execute a final room check to ensure the bathroom is orderly.
Phase 3: The Launch 7:30 AM - 7:45 AM
  • Gear Check: Confirm all bags, laptops, lunches, agendas, phone, wallet, and house keys are at the door. Verify kids' gear.
  • Safety Check: Verify stove is off and coffee maker is unplugged/off.
  • Security: Ensure back door is locked. Suit up for the weather and lock the front door.
Phase 4: Commute 7:45 AM - 8:15 AM
  • Walk to Office: Maintain focus on the commute.
  • Mental Prep: Execute a quick mental review of the workday ahead and monitor local weather conditions.
Phase 5: Office Transition 8:15 AM - 8:30 AM
  • Sustainment: Make coffee at the station for the first deep-work block.
  • Reset: Restroom break before locking in.
  • Workspace: Power up station and clear physical clutter from the desk.
